Black Friday Bemol

Black Friday Theme for App Bemol

Company

Bemol

Type

Product design

Year

2021

Process

Black Friday is one of the most important events for commerce during the year!

The highly anticipated launch of the Bemol app occurred just in time for the biggest shopping event of the year - Black Friday. To celebrate, the app was given a special Black Friday makeover with a themed design that was seamlessly integrated with the overall marketing campaign.

The Black Friday badge was prominently displayed on all discounted products in the app, website, and physical stores, making it easier for shoppers to quickly spot the best deals. This feature not only helped to increase the visibility of the promotions, but it also added an extra level of excitement for shoppers during the festive season.

1 week of discounts on Bemol platforms

Outcome

The Black Friday campaign was a resounding success, resulting in the best product sales in recent years. Feedback surveys showed that customers highly appreciated and praised the Black Friday theme, indicating that it was well received by the Bemol customers.
