Medalhas

Gameified loyalty system using medals earned in different ways.

Company

Bemol

Type

Product design

Year

2021

Process

With the medal program, you participate in a fun game. The more you buy in our physical stores, online through the website, access the free Wi-Fi, purchase insurance services and others, the more Bemol Bonus points you earn. Because from the fourth medal won, you earn 1,000 Bonus points.

The Bemol Card is the best way to buy in stores, kiosks, ChatBot, APP and Bemol website, with fast and hassle-free service. It has numerous advantages and exclusive payment plans. In addition, it entitles the Bemol Client to automatically participate in the Medal Program.

Modernization of old medals

Transforming the old into something new, fun and with benefits.

There was already a medal system, but it was just aesthetic. So there was a discovery focused on how to bring a better experience to the mobile world for Bemol customers.

Discovery

As it was already an existing product, the focus of this discovery was to clarify what had already worked and how we were going to bring it to the app. So we use the following steps: Stakeholder Mapping, What product is, what is not, what product do and do not (É/ Não é, Faz/Não faz), CSD Matrix,, User Journey (and opportunities), Business KPI's, Customers KPI's and OMTM. The Discovery lasted 2 days with the participation of Product Designers, Product Managers, Product Owner, Developers, QAs and Marketing Team.

After discovery, we analyze the development architecture along with refinement of the journey with the prototype.

‍

Outcome

BEMOL BONUS PROGRAM

The user acquire and accumulate points in all their purchases, both on the website and app and in Bemol stores. For every 1,500 points accumulated, you receive a credit from R$10.00 to R$ 20.00 (according to your customer level within the platform) that can be used as a discount on new purchases or payment of installments.

Customers were classified into 5 (five) distinct categories, according to the number of points accumulated in their purchase history, and have different advantages, such as Interest-free plans, longer terms on guarantees purchased in store, free wifi and higher bonus redemption value.
